ABOUT

Lemuria Jewels London is a bespoke jewellery atelier, founded by Tatiana Ovchinnikova in 2014. Influenced by family generations of jewellery-makers, Tatiana and her talented team also take inspiration from the legendary land of Lemuria, a utopia of rare and beautiful gemstones. Creating timeless bespoke pieces that are loved by all is what motivates Lemuria’s artists. And with potential to reinvent and redesign individual pieces over time, a gift from Lemuria Jewels London is genuinely the gift that keeps on giving...

OUR STORY

The legendary land of Lemuria was said to be a place of unparalleled beauty. Considered the closest thing to paradise, Lemuria’s landscape glittered with natural gemstones and its inhabitants enjoyed a peaceful, spiritual existence. When Lemuria eventually descended into the sea, its precious stones were scattered for the world to discover. We at Lemuria Jewels delight in the idea of harnessing unparalleled beauty especially for you – and every piece of jewellery is infused with creativity and imagination unique to our team. If you’re looking for something truly special and meaningful, you’ve come to the right place.
